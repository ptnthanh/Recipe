Instructions

  1. Let the chicken come to room temperature and season with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Preheat a cast iron or non-stick pan over medium heat for 2 minutes with 1 tablespoon of oil.
  3. Add the chicken thighs skin down. Cook for 7-8 minutes or until the skin is deep golden brown.
  4. Flip and cook for another 3-4 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pan when it is cooked through.
  5. Add the chicken stock to the pan with juice from half a lemon and capers. Bring to a simmer.
  6. Cook until the liquid has reduced by half. Add butter and parsley.
  7. Lower heat to low and cook for 1-2 minutes until the sauce has thickened up.
  8. Turn the heat off and set the pan aside.
  9. Plate the chicken and pour over the the lemon pan sauce. Garnish with parsley.
